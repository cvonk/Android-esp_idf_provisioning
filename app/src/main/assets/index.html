Welcome to OPNpool Provisioning

The OPNpool device connects your pool controller to your Wi-Fi network. It keeps a tab on the status of the controller, the pool pump and the chlorinator. It is not only more convenient than walking over to the pool equipment, but will also tell you about problems with e.g. the chlorinator or pump.

This open source/hardware is hosted at https://github.com/cvonk/OPNpool.

The device integrates with Home Assistant, and other home automation systems using the MQTT protocol. You can also access it through http://opnpool.local

⋮Please provision the device using the overflow menu. If your device is already provisioned and you still see this page, then make sure that you are connected to the same subnet as the device.

To do a factory reset, pull GPIO#0 down for 5 seconds.
